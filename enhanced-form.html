Energy

Energy is the ability of to do work

Renewable Energy

This energy comes from natural sources that replenish themselves over short period of
time, making them sustainable for long term use.

Examples

  • Solar Energy
  • Wind Energy
  • Hydropower
  • Biomass Energy

Non-Renewable Energy

This energy comes from sources that do not replenish quickly enough to be sustainable.

Examples

  • Coal
  • Crude Oil
  • Natural Gas
  • Nuclear Fuels

Tables

Feature Renewable Non-Renewable
source sun, wind, water, plants fossil fuels, nuclear fuels
